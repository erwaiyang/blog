Bio

I was born on August 9, 1994 in Taipei, Taiwan.

My father was an instructor in an university in Taiwan. He was interested in computer though he taught traditional Chinese literature.

He created his own teacher’s page using HTML, and wrote a system for recording students’ scores using PHP and MySQL.

I will never forget that moment: My father just changed the word “blue” to “green”, and the whole page turned to a green background. He then changed it to yellow, to gray, and to any common color I had known.

To an elementary school student, that was a magic.

I began my programmer life when I was just 10 or 11. It was in 2005 or 2006.

No blogs, and no social networking websites at that time.

It was the era of personal webpage.

Some Taiwanese children like me wanted a webpage to introduce ourselves. Drawing a table, placing our personal informations on it, and uploaded it to a free web hosting…

It was very fashion though we will undoubtedly think these pages are really ugly today.

Ugly, repeating and messy background. A table without stripped but with a thick border. Without customized hover, visited or active styles, our links were naked-running only with blue and purple.(laugh)

My first website was “熊熊的天地”. “A World of Cute Bears”! Since the young girl liked bears(only toy bears!) very much, her sister and she decided to set up a website and posted their cute toys.

How did a 10-year-old make a page? Microsoft Frontpage? This ancient word might flash on you. But, it was not (the horrible) Frontpage.

“Maybe you are a genius… You made your first page with HTML like your dad?”

No, no, I was not that genius.

It turned out that the dummy girl created her first page with …

Microsoft Word.
